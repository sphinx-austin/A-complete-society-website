What We Offer

To ensure wellbeing and benefit to all students in the society, JKUBSA offer the following:

Excellence

We devote our full energies, talents and committment to the highest quality in all aspects of the society's interest and success of all Biostatistics students.

Team Work

We work together to be more effective, efficient and more fulfilled.
Achieve as a team what individuals cannot, Collaborate towards a common outcome, Celebrate the success of others.
Value diverse talents,initiative and leadeship.
Inspire society's pride and loyalty.

Training

As a society, we offer free training to the registered members of the society on soft statistical skills and softwares such as SPSS, R-Markdown et cetera. These skills make our student skillful and of high demand at the end of their study.
